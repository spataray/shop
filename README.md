# Luxury Shop Calculator 🛍️✨

A classy, bilingual shopping calculator designed for quick discount and tax calculations. Optimized for mobile use while shopping.

## 🌟 Features

- **Thai & English Support**: Defaults to Thai for ease of use.
- **Dynamic Calculation**: Updates results instantly as you type.
- **Configurable Tax**: Toggle tax on/off and set your local rate (remembers your last setting).
- **Luxury Aesthetic**: Elegant gold and charcoal design.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. Open `index.html` in any mobile browser.
2. Enter the **Original Price**.
3. Enter the **Discount %**.
4. (Optional) Toggle **Tax** and adjust the % if needed.
5. See your **Savings** and **Final Price** instantly in the gold results card.
